The Profound Effect of Music

Vocalist, Rachel Lightbody is a young talent based in Glasgow. She graduated Strathclyde University’s BA Applied Music with First Class Honors and now works as a full time vocalist and music tutor.
During her time on the BA Applied music course, Rachel was able to be immersed in many different styles of music, mainly focusing on vocal jazz. Through exposure to these different genres, Rachel’s voice was able to grow into a versatile and mature instrument. It was at the university were she met the musicians who would play beside and inspire her musical movements. These movements have now been able to nourish within the thriving and ever changing live music scene in Glasgow, where Rachel is based. Constantly working with different musicians and groups Rachel is able to tailor her skills and experiences throughout every live performance.
As well as a singer, Rachel has also worked extensively as a teacher, focusing mostly in the areas of singing, songwriting and group work. Working with students ranging from all walks of life. “The energy that is created from a room full of people singing together is second to none. I strive to create that energy in my students everyday, in order to release the profound effects music has on us.”
